## User Story

**As a** coach  
**I want** to establish and manage relationships with my athletes  
**So that** I can assign training units to them and track their progress

**Story Points**: 5

## Description

Coaches need the ability to establish and manage relationships with athletes. This includes sending relationship requests, accepting/declining requests, viewing active relationships, and removing relationships. The system must validate coach-athlete relationships before allowing training unit assignments.

## Technical Notes

- Relationship request: coach sends request to athlete
- Request acceptance: athlete accepts or declines request
- Relationship status: pending, active, declined, removed
- Relationship validation: verify active relationship before assignment
- Relationship removal: soft-delete to maintain historical data
- Notification: notify athletes of relationship requests
- Privacy: respect athlete privacy preferences
