## User Story

**As a** coach  
**I want** to create, view, edit, and archive training units  
**So that** I can build a reusable library of workout templates for my athletes

**Story Points**: 8

## Description

Coaches need the ability to perform full CRUD operations on training units. A training unit consists of a name, ordered list of exercises with sets, reps, distance, rest periods, and notes. Coaches can also specify that the entire exercise sequence should be repeated N times (1-100). Training units are private to the coach by default and can be archived (soft-deleted) to maintain historical data.

## Technical Notes

- Training unit structure: name, exercises (ordered), sets, reps, distance, rest periods, notes
- Repeat/rounds support: entire sequence can be repeated 1-100 times
- Privacy: training units are private to coach by default
- Soft-delete: archive training units instead of hard delete
- Exercise ordering: maintain order of exercises in training unit
- Validation: ensure all required fields are present
