## Description

Support multiple languages and locales to serve international users. Enable users to use the platform in their preferred language with proper locale formatting.

## Business Value

Expands platform accessibility to international users. Improves user experience for non-English speakers and supports global expansion.

## Success Criteria

- English and German translations are available
- Language preference persists across sessions
- Locale formatting works correctly (dates, numbers, etc.)
- UI supports multiple languages seamlessly

## Risks & Mitigation

- **Risk**: Translation quality may vary
  - **Mitigation**: Professional translation review and native speaker validation
- **Risk**: Missing translations may break UI
  - **Mitigation**: Fallback to English and comprehensive translation coverage
