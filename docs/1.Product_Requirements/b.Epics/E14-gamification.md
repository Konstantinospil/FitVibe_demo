## Description

Motivate users through points and badges awarded for completing training sessions. Provide gamification features that enhance user engagement and encourage consistent training.

## Business Value

Increases user engagement and retention by providing rewards and recognition for training achievements. Creates a sense of accomplishment and progress for users.

## Success Criteria

- Users receive points upon session completion
- Badges are awarded for achievements
- Points and badges are visible in user profile
- Gamification features enhance user engagement

## Risks & Mitigation

- **Risk**: Gamification may not motivate all users
  - **Mitigation**: Make gamification optional and focus on meaningful achievements
- **Risk**: Point inflation may reduce value
  - **Mitigation**: Balance point values and provide meaningful rewards
