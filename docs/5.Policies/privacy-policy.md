# FitVibe Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2024-06-01

FitVibe helps athletes plan training sessions, log workouts, and track long-term progress. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information when you interact with the FitVibe websites, mobile applications, APIs, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

## 1. Scope

- Applies to anyone who visits our sites or apps, creates an account, participates in communities, or otherwise uses the Services.
- Covers all personal data processed by FitVibe as controller. When we process data on behalf of teams, clubs, or enterprises, we do so under their instructions and applicable data processing agreements.

## 2. Information we collect

- **Account information:** name, email address, password, authentication tokens, profile photo, and organization/team affiliations.
- **Training and wellness data:** workout logs, performance metrics, training plans, goals, notes, media attachments, and wellness check-ins. Users should avoid storing protected health information unless explicitly permitted in their jurisdiction.
- **Device and technical data:** device identifiers, app version, IP address, browser type, operating system, language, and approximate location derived from IP.
- **Usage data:** activity within the Services, feature interactions, time stamps, diagnostic events, crash reports, and referrer information.
- **Payment data:** if payments are enabled, limited billing details such as transaction identifiers, subscription status, and last four digits of card numbers (payment processors handle full payment credentials).
- **Cookies and similar technologies:** identifiers stored on browsers or devices. See the Cookie Policy for details.
- **Community content:** messages, comments, and other material you post or upload.

## 3. Sources of information

- Directly from you when you create an account, input training data, request support, or participate in surveys.
- Automatically through cookies, SDKs, and analytics tools.
- From organizations that provision accounts or integrate FitVibe with their own systems.
- From third-party services you connect (for example, wearables or calendar providers) with your permission.

## 4. How we use information

- Provide, maintain, and improve the Services, including personalization, recommendations, and training insights.
- Authenticate users, secure accounts, prevent fraud, and protect the integrity of the platform.
- Communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and administrative messages; send marketing communications where permitted by law and your preferences.
- Facilitate collaboration features such as team rosters, leaderboards, and shared training plans.
- Comply with legal obligations, enforce Terms and Conditions/EULA, and protect FitVibe, our users, and the public from harm.

## 5. Legal bases for processing (EEA/UK and similar jurisdictions)

- **Contract:** to deliver the Services you request.
- **Consent:** for optional features (for example, connecting third-party devices, marketing communications, certain cookies). You may withdraw consent at any time.
- **Legitimate interests:** to maintain security, understand usage, develop new features, and prevent abuse, provided your rights and interests are not overridden.
- **Legal obligations:** to meet compliance, accounting, and regulatory requirements.
- **Vital interests or public interest:** if necessary to protect someone’s life or comply with public health directives, subject to applicable law.

## 6. How we share information

- **Service providers:** hosting, analytics, email/SMS delivery, customer support, payment processing, and security vendors under confidentiality and data protection obligations.
- **Integrations and partners:** third-party apps or devices you choose to connect. Data sharing follows your configuration and the partner’s terms.
- **Organizations:** if your account is provisioned by a coach, club, or enterprise, relevant administrators may access certain profile and activity data consistent with their policies.
- **Business transactions:** information may be transferred in the context of a merger, acquisition, financing, or asset sale, subject to confidentiality safeguards and notice where required.
- **Legal and safety:** when required by law, subpoena, or court order, or to protect the rights, property, and safety of FitVibe, users, or the public.
- **Aggregated or de-identified data:** used for analytics and product improvements without identifying individuals.

## 7. International data transfers

- Data may be processed in countries other than where you reside. We implement appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and conduct transfer impact assessments where required.
- For users in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you may request a copy of applicable transfer safeguards via compliance@fitvibe.example.com.

## 8. Data retention

- We retain personal data for as long as needed to provide the Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.
- Criteria include account status, feature usage, contractual requirements, and statutory retention periods. When data is no longer required, we securely delete or de-identify it.

## 9. Security

- FitVibe employs administrative, technical, and physical safeguards such as access controls, encryption in transit, vulnerability management, and security monitoring.
- No system is completely secure; users should choose strong passwords, enable multi-factor authentication when available, and promptly report suspected incidents.

## 10. Your rights

- **Access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, and objection:** available where applicable (e.g., GDPR, UK GDPR, LGPD, PIPEDA). Submit requests to privacy@fitvibe.example.com.
- **Marketing choices:** unsubscribe links are provided in marketing emails; app and browser settings allow you to manage notifications and cookie preferences.
- **California residents:** you may request to know, delete, and correct personal information and opt out of sales or sharing for cross-context behavioral advertising. We do not knowingly sell or share personal information of consumers under 16. Authorized agent requests require verifiable proof of authority.

## 11. Children’s data

- The Services are not directed to children under 16 (or the age required by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without appropriate consent. If you believe a child provided data, contact us to delete it.

## 12. Cross-border health and fitness considerations

- FitVibe provides training and wellness tooling and is not a medical device or a substitute for professional medical advice. Users should consult healthcare professionals before starting new training programs.

## 13. Data controller and contact

- **Controller:** FitVibe (contact: privacy@fitvibe.example.com).
- **Data protection inquiries:** privacy@fitvibe.example.com.
- **Data protection officer (if applicable):** dpo@fitvibe.example.com.
- **EU/UK representative (if appointed):** contact details will be published in localized supplements.

## 14. Changes to this policy

We will update this policy when our practices change or as required by law. Material changes will be communicated through the Services, email, or other reasonable means. The "Effective Date" above will be updated, and prior versions will be archived.

## 15. How to exercise your rights

Submit requests or questions by emailing privacy@fitvibe.example.com with your name, contact information, and a description of your request. We may ask for additional verification to protect your account.
